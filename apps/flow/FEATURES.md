# Flow Portal (FlowPortal / SME) — Implemented Features

A running list of features built in the Flow portal. Updated as each new feature ships.

1. **Authentication** — Sign up, log in, log out, email verification, and password reset.
2. **Organisation** — Create and manage your organisation's profile, details, and logo.
3. **Organisation switching** — Switch between organisations you belong to (same portal type).
4. **Invitations** — Invite team members to your organisation and manage pending invites.
5. **Profile** — View and update your personal account details.
6. **Notifications** — In-app notifications panel.
7. **Levy eligibility check** — A public, no-login quiz that tells a small employer whether they qualify for apprenticeship levy transfer funding, with an estimated funding band and a link to start registration.
8. **ESFA registration wizard** — A public, resumable 5-step onboarding wizard (company verification, PAYE, DAS account, bank details, consent) that sets up an employer's Digital Apprenticeship Service account before they have a login.
9. **AI programme catalogue** — Browse the platform-curated FlowPortal AI apprenticeship tracks and view each programme's module outline.
10. **Enrol an apprentice** — Enrol an apprentice onto an AI programme in one step (creating the learner if needed).
11. **Learner module progress** — Track an apprentice through their programme modules with a progress bar, and mark the programme complete once every module is done.
12. **SME dashboard (Growth hub)** — A mobile-friendly home screen with live KPIs (active apprentices, pending approvals, reviews due, funding status), a commitment pipeline overview, a "needs your approval" list, and your apprentice roster — each deep-linking into the relevant action.
13. **OTJ approvals** — Review the off-the-job training hours your apprentices submit and approve or reject them in bulk, with an optional reason.
14. **Reviews** — Schedule, record, and sign off tripartite progress reviews, with a month calendar view.
15. **Funding (DAS)** — Your levy balance with manual sync, a spend forecast and runway estimate, and a table of synced funding payments (with clawbacks flagged).
16. **Commitment statements** — Create, publish, and collect signatures on the tripartite training plan for each apprentice (managed from the apprentice's page), plus a portfolio-wide overview table.
