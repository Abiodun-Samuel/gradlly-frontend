# Provider Portal — Implemented Features

A running list of features built in the provider portal. Updated as each new feature ships.

1. **Authentication** — Sign up, log in, log out, email verification, and password reset.
2. **Organisation** — Create and manage your organisation's profile, details, and logo.
3. **Organisation switching** — Switch between organisations you belong to (same portal type).
4. **Invitations** — Invite team members to your organisation and manage pending invites.
5. **Profile** — View and update your personal account details.
6. **Notifications** — In-app notifications panel.
7. **Programmes** — Create and manage your top-level apprenticeship programmes.
8. **Standards** — Manage apprenticeship standards under each programme.
9. **KSB Definitions** — Define the Knowledge, Skills & Behaviours for each standard.
10. **Apprentices** — Manage the learners in your organisation.
11. **Enrolments** — Enrol apprentices onto standards and track each one's lifecycle and journey.
12. **Commitment Statements** — Create, publish, and collect signatures on the tripartite training plan for each enrolment.
13. **OTJ Log Entries** — Review and approve/reject the off-the-job training hours apprentices submit.
14. **Reviews** — Schedule, record, and sign off tripartite progress reviews, with a calendar view.
15. **Portfolio & KSB Coverage** — Review evidence, track KSB coverage, and generate the EPA pack for end-point assessment.
16. **E-Signature** — Sign a standalone PDF document.
17. **Ofsted Hub (Quality / EIF)** — Inspection-readiness dashboard, quality improvement plan, safeguarding checklist, programme documents, and the inspector evidence pack.
18. **Learners (Caseload)** — Cohort dashboard with filters and CSV export, an at-risk intervention queue with employer outreach, logging tutor interventions, and a 360° learner profile.
